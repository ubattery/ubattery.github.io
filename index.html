uBattery is a powerful battery monitoring tool designed to help you clearly understand the real condition of your laptop or UPS battery. It is built to be lightweight and efficient, so it can run smoothly even on older computers without slowing down your system. At the same time, it provides detailed and accurate information that is usually only available in more complex tools.

What makes uBattery different is how it works behind the scenes. Instead of showing estimated or adjusted values, it reads data directly from the battery hardware and system sensors. This means the information you see reflects the actual state of your battery in real time, without artificial calculations or smoothing. Because of this, you can rely on the data to better understand how your battery is performing in everyday use.

uBattery displays important details such as battery capacity, voltage, temperature, charging and discharging activity, as well as a live graph that shows how your battery behaves over time. These features make it easier to spot changes, identify unusual behavior, and understand how different activities affect power usage.

In addition, uBattery includes a built-in remote web server, allowing you to monitor your battery from another device through a web browser. For more advanced users, a JSON API is also available, making it possible to use the data for custom applications or monitoring systems.

Overall, uBattery is designed to be easy to use while still providing meaningful and reliable information. Whether you simply want to check your battery status or take a deeper look at its health and behavior, uBattery gives you the tools to understand it more clearly and make better decisions in everyday use.
